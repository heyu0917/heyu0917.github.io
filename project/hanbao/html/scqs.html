The third pole of the catering industry

小吃简快餐饮竞争激烈

外卖大爆发成为餐饮业第三极

从门店数量来看，小吃简快餐、面包甜点、火锅、川菜、烧烤五大品类门店数排名靠前，其中小吃简快餐类门店占餐饮门店总数的近1/3。面包甜点门店也随着休闲场景而兴起，尤其在一线城市最为密集，广州有超过26000家面包甜点店，为全国最高。

互联网的力量正在给传统的餐饮行业带来新的增长。2016年全国餐饮行业的增速为10%，但互联网餐饮的增长却高达300%。报告显示，2016年外卖行业总体交易额超过1500亿，占餐饮业总体大盘的4%，增速迅猛。外卖已经成为继做饭和堂食之后国人的第三种常规就餐方式。而外卖消费也已经超出正餐的范畴，美团外卖有25%的订单量产生在早餐、下午茶、夜宵等非正餐时段。

From the number of stores, snack snacks, snack, bread, dessert, chafing dish, Sichuan cuisine and barbecue five stores ranked the top. Snack bars and fast food restaurants accounted for nearly 1/3 of the total number of restaurants. Bakery shops are also emerging with leisure scenes, especially in the first tier cities, with more than 26000 bakery desserts in Guangzhou, the highest in the country.

The power of the Internet is bringing new growth to the traditional catering industry. The growth of the national catering industry in 2016 was 10%, but the growth of Internet catering was up to 300%. The report shows that in 2016, the total trade volume of the takeout industry was over 150 billion, accounting for 4% of the overall catering trade market and rapid growth. Takeout has become the third conventional way of eating after cooking and dining. And takeout consumption has gone beyond the scope of the meal, with 25% of the order produced in the breakfast, afternoon tea, night night, and other non - meal periods.
